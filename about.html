Craft Meets Technology

I'm Isaac, a maker with a background in engineering design who enjoys combining traditional craft with modern tools. My 3D printed products often grow out of practical needs in the workshop, where I look for simple, reliable solutions that make everyday tasks easier.

I take care to design with clarity and usability in mind, drawing on both technical training and hands‑on experience. Documenting the process is part of the work for me—it helps keep ideas transparent and makes it easier for others to build on them.

I'm always interested in new challenges, so if there's something you think would help you turn, I'd love to hear from you. Let's bring your idea to life.
